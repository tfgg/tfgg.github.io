A cap on net migration

A cap on net migration means limiting the total number of people coming to the UK every year, minus those leaving. This reflects the change in population of the UK ignoring births and deaths.

In 2010 the Conservative's stated goal was a cap of 100,000. Below is a graph of the net migration to the UK every year for the last 10 years.
